Schedule Sports Events

Sanggahan! allows users to schedule events days ahead. Plan your team practice, or create a game that those in your area can join. You can design events in-depth, from the players invited, to what venue you wish to play at.

Follow Games

If you play a game scheduled through the Sanggahan! system, all information about that game will be recorded into the database for everyone to see. Track your games and see who was the MVP, or see how you're progressing in your selected sport.
