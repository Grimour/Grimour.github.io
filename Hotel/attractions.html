The Citylight Attractions

Food, Bar, and Coffee

Eat Up

Fine Dining

Salt and Pepper Restaurant

Known for its breakfast and lunch buffet, Citylight Hotel’s Salt and Pepper Restaurant serves mouth-watering buffet and Ala Carte Dinner from Mondays to Sundays. Get to experience sumptuous Filipino Breakfast Buffet from 6:30am to 10:00am and Asian Lunch Buffet from 11:30pm to 2:00pm with your family and friends! For a more intimate dining experience join us for our Ala Carte Dinner from 6:00pm to 10:00pm.



The Bar

Current, fresh, hip and dynamic- the Citylight Hotel bar is always in full swing, from your day's end up until midnight. With a wide variety of cocktails, wines, beers and even non-alcoholic beverages, it definitely is the ideal hangout for all those who want to let their hair down or feel footloose after a day of demanding activities. With great hip music, the Citylight Hotel bar atmosphere is constantly up and high with energy and enjoyment! So take a sip and experience coolness and fun at the Citylight Hotel bar with your friends and family!



The Coffee Shop

Enjoy freshly baked breads, cakes, sandwiches and delectable pastas at Citylight Hotel’s coffeeshop. Our premium teas and coffee brews are the perfect combination to Baguio’s cool breeze or our shakes and smoothies to freshen you up on a hot summer day. The Coffee Shop is the perfect spot to chill and savour a sweet slice of cake.
