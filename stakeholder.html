Stakeholder FAQ

Do you want to join Sanggahan?

How do I join?

First, register your business into the system, and present all necessary permits to Mr. Sagsago in the University of Baguio. If you are approved, you will promptly be interviewed before being officially affiliated as a Sanggahan! stakeholder

What services do I provide?

Sanggahan! supports businesses that retail sports equipment and are comfortable with the concept of rental, as well as venue providers who have safe, quality courts and gyms.

What are my responsibilities?

Operate your business as usual, but be on the look out for Sanggahan! clients who wish to avail our services through your establishment.

How does the profit split?

50-50
