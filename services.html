Sanggahan! brings sports to your phone and computer

Communicate

Talk to your teammates no matter when and where with our dedicated messaging feature. Contact individual members of your team, arrange a team meeting with everyone, or simply join the public chat, segregated into different servers depending on your sport, and truly talk to your community!

Schedule

Create both public and private events where you can invite friends and rivals to play with, or to simply schedule team practices in preparation of a nearing competition. With the dedicated support of Sanggahan's stakeholders, you can set these events at your local, supported sports venue!

Game

With our live score tracking feature, as well as in-depth reports of current ongoing games and past games, you can follow and participate in games all over the Philippines. Follow your favorite player, or even track your own progress by creating a profile on our mobile application!
