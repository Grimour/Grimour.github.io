Who are we?

Sanggahan! is a start-up company currently in development in the IT Department of the University of Baguio.

History

Developed by the team of Beltran, Beninsig, Mejia, and Sagsago, Sanggahan's start began June 2018 for the team's Project Study class. With the help of their Technical Advisor, Miss Carpiso, as well as their three panelists, the project hopes to come into fruition in late October. Currently, stakeholders are mainly the sports venues and outlets in the Baguio area, but the team hopes to expand the project to encompass the entire Philippine area.

Technical Information

Sanggahan's website was conceptualized by Sagsago, designed by Beninsig and Mejia, and programmed by Beltran. The language used is PHP in Laravel, and the database uses MySQL provided by xampp. Lastly, the design is developed through Bootstrap, and online hosting through HostGator.
